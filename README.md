# 💫 About Me:
I am a Data Science Engineer passionate about building intelligent systems that turn complex data into meaningful impact. With three years of experience across machine learning, AI, data engineering, and software development, I have worked on projects ranging from predictive modeling and NLP applications to large-scale data integration pipelines. My most recent work involved developing an AI-driven malaria outbreak forecasting system in collaboration with GIZ, combining satellite imagery, environmental factors, and socio-economic indicators to support real-world decisions. I thrive in environments where technology, experimentation, and problem-solving come together to create practical solutions.
